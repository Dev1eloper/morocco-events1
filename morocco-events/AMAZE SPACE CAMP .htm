AMAZE SPACE CAMP

About the camp

AMAZE yourself at our space-themed camp in Morocco! AMAZE Space Camp offers an unparalleled opportunity for students aged 15-18 to immerse themselves in the world of space exploration. With expert instructors, our program provides hands-on learning, interactive sessions, space simulations and an analog mission that will ignite your passion for science and technology. Discover the thrill of launching rockets, conducting experiments, and simulating space missions as you embark on an unforgettable adventure at AMAZE: The Astronaut Mission Adventure Zone in Morocco.

Before applying, please ensure that you meet the following eligibility criteria:

  • Be born between August 29, 2005, and August 28, 2008.
  • Student in High school: Common Core, First or Second year Bac, or University during the 2022-2023 academic year.
  • Moroccan citizen residing in Morocco.
  • English Language Proficiency: The AMAZE Space Camp program is conducted entirely in English.
  • Be available to travel to camp location from August 27th to Sept 2nd 2023.
  • Interest in Space and STEAM fields: You should have a keen interest in STEAM (Science,Technology, Engineering, Art or Mathematics). Previous experience in STEAM fields can be advantageous, although it is not a requirement.
  • Inclusivity and Accessibility: We welcome applications from all eligible individuals, and strongly encourage people with physical disabilities to apply.
