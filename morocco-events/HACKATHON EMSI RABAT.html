NEXT-GEN HACKATHON EMSI RABAT

Groupe Emsi in Rabat, along with GDSC EMSI RABAT, is thrilled to announce the inaugural edition of the EMSI NEXT-GEN HACKATHON.

This exciting hackathon will take place from July 21 to 23, 2023, at EMSI Agdal 2, located at Avenue Oqba, Angle Rue Assouhaili, Rabat 10000. The event holds special significance as it coincides with EMSI's celebration of 37 years of excellence in engineering education.

Since its establishment in 1986, EMSI has been at the forefront of engineering education in Morocco, producing generations of talented engineers who have played a vital role in the country's technological and economic advancement.

In honor of this remarkable milestone, EMSI and GDSC EMSI are organizing the NEXT-GEN HACKATHON, an opportunity for students from various engineering disciplines and Moroccan schools to come together and showcase their innovative prowess.

This hackathon aims to foster collaboration, creativity, and problem-solving skills among the participating students. It will serve as a platform for budding engineers to tackle real-world challenges and develop groundbreaking solutions.

The event promises to create a dynamic environment for learning, networking, and sharing ideas by bringing together young minds from diverse backgrounds.

In this event Students will have the chance to compete in teams, showcasing their technical skills and expertise in software development, artificial intelligence, the Internet of Things (IoT), and much more.

Don't miss this incredible opportunity to witness the convergence of engineering excellence, creativity, and ingenuity at the NEXT-GEN HACKATHON EMSI RABAT.

Join us from July 21 to 23, 2023, as we celebrate the spirit of innovation and engineering prowess in Morocco.
